# Code of Conduct

## Purpose
To promote a respectful and professional workplace.

## Expected Behavior
- Respect all colleagues
- Maintain professionalism
- Communicate effectively

## Prohibited Behavior
- Harassment or discrimination
- Violence or threats
- Substance abuse at work

## Diversity and Inclusion
We are committed to an inclusive workplace for all.

## Reporting Misconduct
Employees may report concerns confidentially.

## Disciplinary Action
Violations may result in warnings, suspension, or termination.
