# Device Usage Policy

## Purpose
To ensure secure and appropriate use of company devices.

## Acceptable Use
- Work-related activities
- Limited personal use (non-disruptive)

## Prohibited Use
- Illegal activities
- Downloading unauthorized software
- Accessing inappropriate content

## Security
- Devices must be password protected
- Automatic lock after 5 minutes of inactivity

## Monitoring
The company reserves the right to monitor usage.

## Loss or Theft
Employees must report lost or stolen devices immediately.

## Return of Equipment
All devices must be returned upon termination.
