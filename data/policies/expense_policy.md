# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Purpose
To ensure proper management and reimbursement of business-related expenses.

## Eligible Expenses
- Travel (flights, accommodation)
- Meals during business travel
- Office supplies
- Client-related expenses

## Submission
- Expenses must be submitted within 14 days.
- Receipts are required for all claims.

## Approval
- Manager approval is required for expenses above $100.

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses
- Personal purchases
- Alcohol (unless approved for client events)
- Late fees or fines

## Payment
Reimbursements are processed within 10 business days.
