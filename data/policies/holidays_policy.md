# Holidays Policy

## Overview
Employees are entitled to public holidays recognized by the company.

## Standard Holidays
- New Year's Day
- Human Rights Day
- Freedom Day
- Workers' Day
- Youth Day
- Heritage Day
- Day of Reconciliation
- Christmas Day

## Additional Leave
- Employees may request additional unpaid leave for cultural or religious observances.

## Holiday Work
Employees required to work on holidays will receive:
- Overtime pay OR
- Time off in lieu

## Regional Differences
Holiday schedules may vary by region.
