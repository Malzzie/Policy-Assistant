# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

## Overview
This Paid Time Off (PTO) policy provides employees with paid leave for vacation, personal matters, and illness.

## Eligibility
All full-time employees are eligible for PTO. Part-time employees receive prorated PTO based on hours worked.

## Accrual
- Employees accrue PTO monthly.
- Accrual rates:
  - 0–2 years: 15 days annually
  - 3–5 years: 18 days annually
  - 5+ years: 22 days annually

## Usage
- PTO may be used for vacation, personal leave, or illness.
- Requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance (except emergencies).

## Carryover
- Employees may carry over up to 5 unused days into the next year.
- Excess PTO will be forfeited unless otherwise approved.

## Sick Leave
- Sick leave is included within PTO.
- Extended medical leave may require documentation.

## Termination
Unused PTO will be paid out upon termination, subject to local labor laws.
