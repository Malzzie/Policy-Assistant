# Remote Work Policy

## Purpose
To define expectations and guidelines for employees working remotely.

## Eligibility
Remote work is available to employees whose roles permit it and with manager approval.

## Work Hours
- Employees must be available between 09:00–15:00 (core hours).
- Flexible scheduling is allowed outside of core hours.

## Communication
- Daily check-ins via Slack or Teams.
- Weekly team meetings are mandatory.

## Productivity
Employees are expected to meet the same performance standards as in-office staff.

## Equipment
- Company-issued devices must be used.
- Secure internet connection required.

## Security
- VPN usage is mandatory.
- Confidential data must not be stored on personal devices.

## Compliance
Failure to comply may result in revocation of remote work privileges.
