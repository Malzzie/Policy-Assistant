EMPLOYEE SECURITY & COMPLIANCE GUIDE
-----------------------------------

Company Confidential

1. Introduction
This document outlines security and compliance expectations for all employees.

2. Data Handling
- All sensitive data must be encrypted.
- Access must be restricted to authorized personnel only.

3. Acceptable Use
- Systems must only be used for business purposes.
- Unauthorized access attempts are prohibited.

4. Incident Response
- Report incidents immediately.
- Follow escalation procedures.

5. Compliance
- Adhere to GDPR and local data protection laws.

6. Acknowledgment
All employees must sign acknowledgment of this policy.

End of Document
